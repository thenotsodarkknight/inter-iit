Our Vision.

Inter IIT Tech Meet saw its humble beginning in 2012 at IIT Kanpur as Pan IIT Student Initiative and now has grown into annual meet approved by IIT Council with participation from 17 IITs encompassing 500+ students in the 6th edition at IIT Madras. Primarily the Inter IIT would have collaborative events targeting networking and knowledge sharing and competitive events which fueling innovative work with given constraints to meet the mission and vision of Tech Meet.

Inter IIT Tech Meets are envisioned in challenging participants to come up with technology solutions for the problems, compete with teams in a healthy environment, interact with like-minded people across the country and promote transfer of this technology to ventures which will address problems at grass-root levels.
